# 🧔 Stock-N-Stache

**Liquor inventory management that actually works.**

No more "the book." No more guessing. No more losing money to theft or unpaid debts.

## The Problem
Kenyan liquor shop owners lose thousands every month because:
- Stock isn't tracked (you don't know what's gone)
- Customers buy on credit and forget to pay
- No cash to restock = death spiral

## The Solution
Stock-N-Stache fixes all of it.

✅ **Real-time inventory** — Know exactly what you have, what's low, what's selling  
✅ **Debt book on steroids** — Every customer, every shilling, with M-Pesa STK push  
✅ **ETIMS ready** — KRA compliance without the headache (Pro plan)  
✅ **Multi-store** — Manage 1 or 10 shops from one dashboard
